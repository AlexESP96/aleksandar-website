RAD SA MNOM

Mentalno zdravlje u radnom okruženju za organizacije, HR timove i profesionalce.

Dva jasna puta: organizaciono blagostanje za HR timove i međunarodne kompanije kao primarni fokus, i individualna podrška za profesionalce koji prolaze kroz moderan profesionalni pritisak kao sekundarna putanja.

Kako sarađujem sa organizacijama

Podrška zaposlenima

Poverljiva, strukturisana podrška za zaposlene koji se suočavaju sa stresom, emocionalnom preopterećenošću, adaptacijom ili izazovima na poslu.

Burnout and resilience

Radionice and sessions focused on stress, recovery, boundaries, emotional regulation, and sustainable performance.

Leadership wellbeing

Podrška for founders, managers, and team leads navigating pressure, decision-making, communication, and responsibility.

Tailored workshops

Practical sessions for teams on wellbeing, communication, psychological safety, change, and workplace culture.

How it works

A simple, professional process

Choose the right option

Book an individual session or schedule a workplace wellbeing consultation for your organization.

Meet online

Sessions can take place online. Individual in-person sessions may be available in Barcelona, and company sessions can be arranged in person depending on the format.

Define next steps

Za profesionalce, we focus on your current needs. Za organizacije, we clarify the most useful format, package, and scope.

Open to collaborations

Available for selected collaborations with clinics, organizations, EAP providers, coworking communities, and wellbeing initiatives aligned with grounded psychological support.
