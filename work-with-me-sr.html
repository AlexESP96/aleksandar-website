How it works

A simple, professional process

Choose the right option

Book an individual session or schedule a radno mental health and organizaciono blagostanje consultation for your organization.

Meet online

Sessions can take place online. Individual in-person sessions may be available in Barcelona, and company sessions can be arranged in person depending on the format.

Define next steps

For professionals, we focus on your current needs. Za organizacije, we clarify the most useful format, package, and scope.

Open to organizaciono collaborations

Available for selected collaborations with HR teams, organizations, EAP providers and organizaciono blagostanje initiatives aligned with evidence-informed psychologically informed support.
