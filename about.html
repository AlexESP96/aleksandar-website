About

A grounded, evidence-based approach to everyday pressure.

My background is in psychology, with a strong focus on mental health at work, human behavior, and practical support for people navigating pressure, uncertainty, and professional demands.

My work combines academic training, research experience, and years of exposure to organizational environments. I aim to make psychological knowledge useful, structured, and accessible without making support feel cold or clinical.

Professional background

Psychology, research, and the realities of modern work.

My current academic work includes finishing a PhD in Psychology at the Universitat de València, with research focused on employee wellbeing, burnout, inclusive employment, leadership, and workplace mental health.

More than five years of industry experience through VeraSafe has given me close exposure to international, remote-first teams and people-related processes. This brings a practical understanding of pressure, performance, communication, decision-making, and the everyday challenges professionals face.

Direct support experience also shapes my work, including over a year providing psychological counseling support and four years as a suicide prevention officer. These roles strengthened the way I approach sensitive conversations: with structure, care, and respect for the person in front of me.

Support is available in Serbian, English, Spanish, and conversational Italian where appropriate. Being able to express yourself in your native or most comfortable language can make a real difference, especially when discussing pressure, emotions, and personal experiences.

The work is designed for individuals and organizations that want support that is calm, structured, and grounded in psychological science. The style is direct, warm, and practical: the goal is not to overcomplicate your life, but to help you understand what is happening and make better choices from there.
