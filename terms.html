Terms & Conditions

Clear terms for using this website and engaging with psychological support, workplace wellbeing and organizational services.

Services offered

Services may include individual psychological support, introductory consultations, workplace wellbeing consultations, workshops, speaking engagements, referral conversations and organizational collaboration.

Individual psychological support

Individual support focuses on work stress, burnout risk, adjustment, relocation, emotional wellbeing, identity and life transitions, and professional functioning. It is not emergency, psychiatric or regulated sanitary psychological treatment in Spain.

Workplace wellbeing and organizations

Organizational work may include workshops, leadership wellbeing sessions, employee support pathways, consultations and tailored wellbeing initiatives. Scope, format, timing and deliverables are agreed before work begins.

Booking, payment and cancellation

Bookings are made through the scheduling system. Paid sessions are confirmed according to the booking and payment process. Cancellation or rescheduling terms may vary by service and will be communicated during booking or agreement.

Online sessions

Online sessions require a private space, stable internet connection and appropriate technology. Clients are responsible for joining on time and ensuring they are in a suitable environment.

Confidentiality and limits

Information shared is treated respectfully and confidentially, subject to legal, ethical and safety limits. If there is serious risk of harm or emergency need, more appropriate services may be recommended or contacted where required.

Intellectual property

Website content, workshop materials, visuals, text and resources are protected and may not be copied, adapted or reused without permission unless explicitly stated.

Changes and contact

These terms may be updated as services evolve. For questions, contact info@aleksandarplavsic.com.
