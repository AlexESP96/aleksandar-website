Support for professional and personal pressure.

Individual psychological support is available online and, where possible, in Barcelona. The work is structured, respectful and adapted to your context.

Anxiety and work stressSupport for emotional overload, uncertainty, performance pressure and difficult routines.
Career transitionsChanging roles, identity shifts, confidence, decisions and professional direction.
Workplace conflictDifficult conversations, communication strain, boundaries and interpersonal stress.
