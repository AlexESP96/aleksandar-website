How can Aleksandar help me?

What Individual Support Can Help With

Individual psychological support is available online and, where possible, in Barcelona. The work focuses on real-life professional and personal pressure rather than diagnosis-heavy labels.

Feeling overwhelmed by work demandsUnderstanding pressure, stress patterns, recovery and boundaries before stress becomes burnout.
Understanding stress patternsSupport for emotional overload, uncertainty, performance pressure and difficult routines.
Rebuilding confidence after changeCareer transitions, life changes, identity shifts, decisions and professional direction.
Workplace conflictDifficult conversations, communication strain, boundaries and interpersonal stress.
Navigating relocation or life abroadRelocation, language, belonging, loneliness and adapting to life in a new country.
Healthier boundaries and routinesBalancing personal wellbeing with professional responsibilities, expectations and relationships.

Formal Education

A foundation across clinical psychology, organizational psychology and workplace mental health.

My background combines clinical psychology, organizational psychology and workplace mental health research. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Clinical Psychology, a Master's Degree in Industrial and Organizational Psychology, and I am currently a PhD candidate researching workplace mental health and wellbeing.

Why this may be a good fit

Support informed by counselling experience, workplace understanding and research.

I have two years of experience providing psychological counselling and individual support, four years of suicide prevention and emotional support experience, and more than five years in international corporate environments. This helps me understand both the personal and workplace dimensions of wellbeing.

For clinics, psychologists and referral partners

Why clinics and psychologists may refer to me

Clinics, psychologists, counsellors, HR professionals, coaches, EAP providers and wellbeing partners may refer adults and professionals who would benefit from psychologically informed support around work stress, burnout risk, adjustment, relocation, professional pressure and emotional wellbeing.

My background combines a Bachelor's Degree in Clinical Psychology, approximately two years of psychological counselling experience, four years connected to suicide-prevention and emotional support work, organizational psychology training, international workplace experience and PhD research in workplace mental health.

This combination is especially useful for clients whose concerns sit between personal wellbeing and working life: international professionals, expats, people adjusting to a new country, and adults navigating workplace pressure, burnout risk or professional transitions.
