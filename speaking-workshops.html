Organizations & workshops

Psychologically informed support for demanding environments.

Workshops, talks and collaboration formats for HR teams, People & Culture teams, managers, leadership groups, international organizations, clinics and wellbeing initiatives.

Workshop topics

Psychologically informed support for burnout prevention, workplace pressure and organizational wellbeing.

Sessions can be adapted for Heads of HR, People & Culture teams, Talent teams, managers, leadership groups, distributed teams and organizational wellbeing initiatives.

Burnout prevention before crisis

A workshop on recognizing workload pressure, emotional exhaustion, stress patterns and early warning signs before burnout escalates.

  • Learning outcomes
  • Recognize early burnout indicators
  • Understand how workload pressure accumulates
  • Identify stress patterns before crisis
  • Connect recovery practices with sustainable performance

Emotional regulation under pressure

A practical session on how stress affects attention, reactions, communication, judgment and decision-making in demanding work environments.

  • Learning outcomes
  • Understand the stress response at work
  • Recognize emotional escalation patterns
  • Use regulation tools during difficult moments
  • Improve communication under pressure

Workplace wellbeing for international and multicultural teams

A session for international teams exploring workload pressure, psychological safety, remote work, cultural complexity and healthier workplace cultures.

  • Learning outcomes
  • Recognize wellbeing risks in distributed teams
  • Improve communication across cultures
  • Strengthen psychological safety
  • Clarify healthier team norms

Leadership resilience

A workshop for leaders, managers and team leads carrying responsibility for people, decisions, uncertainty and team wellbeing.

  • Learning outcomes
  • Understand leadership pressure and emotional load
  • Recognize decision fatigue and responsibility strain
  • Develop practical resilience strategies
  • Support teams without neglecting personal wellbeing

Psychological sustainability at work

A structured session on building work patterns that support performance while respecting recovery, boundaries and human limits.

  • Learning outcomes
  • Understand psychological sustainability at work
  • Identify unsustainable work patterns
  • Improve boundaries and recovery practices
  • Connect wellbeing with long-term performance

The People Behind the People: Mental Health and Resilience for HR Professionals

A focused workshop for HR professionals, Talent Acquisition teams, People & Culture teams, HR Business Partners and Employee Experience teams.

  • Example topics
  • Burnout in HR
  • Emotional load of people-facing roles
  • Managing difficult conversations
  • Psychological boundaries at work
  • Supporting others without neglecting yourself
