Sa kim radim

Podrška za ljude i timove koji prolaze kroz pritisak, promene i zahtevna okruženja.

Radim sa pojedincima, profesionalcima, liderima i organizacijama koje traže psihološki informisanu podršku koja je praktična, strukturisana i ljudska.

Individuals

Za ljude koji žele da razumeju svoje emocije, upravljaju pritiskom, poboljšaju donošenje odluka i izgrade zdravije navike.

  • Stres i preplavljenost
  • Emotional clarity
  • Personal transitions
  • Samopouzdanje i samorazumevanje

Professionals

For people working in demanding roles who need space to think clearly, regulate pressure, and make better choices.

  • Burnout prevention
  • Work pressure
  • Focus and routines
  • Career-related stress

Leaders & managers

For people responsible for decisions, teams, and communication who need grounded support without generic leadership clichés.

  • Decision-making
  • Team communication
  • Emotional load of leadership
  • Resilience

Organizations

For companies that want practical wellbeing support, workshops, and psychologically informed initiatives for their people.

  • Workplace wellbeing
  • Resilience sessions
  • Workshops
  • Employee support

This may be a good fit if you want...

clearer thinking, better emotional awareness, more sustainable routines, and support that connects wellbeing with real-life work and personal demands.
