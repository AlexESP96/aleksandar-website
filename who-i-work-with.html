Who I Work With

For people and teams carrying more pressure than they show.

You may be managing responsibilities on the outside while feeling tired, tense, overwhelmed, or disconnected on the inside. I work with individuals and organizations looking for support that helps them better understand that pressure and respond to it in a healthier, more sustainable way.

Individuals

For people who want space to slow down, better understand what they are feeling, and make sense of stress, uncertainty, or emotional overwhelm.

  • Stress and overwhelm
  • Emotional clarity
  • Self-understanding
  • Online or in person in Barcelona

Professionals

For people whose work pressure is beginning to affect their energy, focus, confidence, mood, or ability to properly switch off.

  • Burnout prevention
  • Pressure and performance strain
  • Decision-making under stress
  • Healthier professional routines

Leaders & managers

For people who support others while also carrying significant responsibility themselves, and need space to think, communicate, and make decisions more clearly.

  • Leadership pressure
  • Team communication
  • Emotional steadiness
  • Clearer, calmer decisions

Organizations

For companies that want wellbeing support that feels practical, human, and relevant to how people actually experience pressure at work.

  • Workplace wellbeing
  • Stress and burnout prevention
  • Workshops and talks
  • Online or in person by arrangement

This may be a good fit if you want...

support that connects emotional wellbeing with the realities of work, responsibility, pressure, communication, and change. The aim is not to label everything or reduce wellbeing to productivity advice, but to understand what is happening and build a healthier, more sustainable way forward.
