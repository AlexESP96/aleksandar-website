Who I work with

For people and teams carrying more pressure than they show.

I work with individuals and organizations looking for psychological support that helps them understand pressure, emotional overwhelm, and stress more clearly, and respond in a healthier, more sustainable way.

Individuals

For local and international clients navigating emotional overwhelm, stress, difficult periods, relationships, identity, self-understanding, or pressure connected to work and everyday life.

  • Stress and emotional overwhelm
  • Work pressure and burnout risk
  • Relationships, identity, and communication
  • Life transitions and uncertainty
  • LGBTQ+ friendly support
  • Online or in person in Barcelona

Organizations & teams

For companies, clinics, leaders, HR teams, and wellbeing initiatives looking for psychologically informed support around pressure, burnout prevention, communication, and the human side of performance.

  • Workplace wellbeing
  • Stress and burnout prevention
  • Leadership pressure and communication
  • Employee support and wellbeing culture
  • Workshops, talks, and wellbeing sessions
  • Online or in person by arrangement

This may be a good fit if you want...

support that connects emotional wellbeing with the realities of work, relationships, responsibility, pressure, communication, and change. The aim is not to label everything or reduce wellbeing to productivity advice, but to better understand what is happening and build a healthier, more sustainable way forward.
