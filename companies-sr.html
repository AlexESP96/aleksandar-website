FOR ORGANIZATIONS

Mentalno zdravlje u profesionalnom okruženju & performance sustainability
for international and multicultural teams.

Psychologically informed support for HR, People & Culture and wellbeing teams addressing burnout risk, pritisak liderstva, interkulturalna komunikacija and sustainable psychological functioning in modern international work environments.

Razgovor o saradnji

Organizational offer

Šta nudim

Radionice i predavanja, dobrobit lidera sessions, EAP-style employee support, psychoeducation and advisory collaboration around radno mental health.

Za koga je

HR, People & Culture, wellbeing leads, L&D teams, clinics, coworking communities and međunarodne organizacije with distributed or multicultural teams.

Izazovi koji se adresiraju

Burnout risk, radno pressure, emotional exhaustion, communication strain, change fatigue, remote-work pressure and liderstvo overload.

Kako funkcioniše saradnja

We clarify the audience, radno context and goals, then define a format: one-off workshop, series, liderstvo session, employee support pathway or wellbeing initiative.

A serious, psychologically informed approach to organizaciono blagostanje.

Strukturisane usluge mentalnog zdravlja u profesionalnom okruženju za HR, liderske timove i međunarodne organizacije koje se suočavaju sa rizikom od burnouta, organizacionim promenama, pritiskom i psihološkom održivošću.

Najčešći izazovi u radnom okruženju

  • Burnout risk and emotional exhaustion before they become performance or retention issues.
  • Workload pressure, unclear boundaries, availability expectations and remote-work strain.
  • Leadership pressure, decision fatigue and people responsibility in changing environments.
  • Cross-cultural communication, adaptation stress and trust-building in multicultural teams.
  • Psychological safety, difficult conversations, conflict patterns and team communication under stress.
  • Employee wellbeing in international, hybrid, distributed or high-responsibility environments.

Formati radionica i treninga

  • 60–90 minute introductory structured workshops for teams and liderstvo groups, communities or liderstvo groups.
  • Multi-session radno mental health and organizaciono blagostanje series focused on prevencija burnouta and sustainable performance.
  • Manager and liderstvo sessions on pressure, communication, emotional regulation and team wellbeing.
  • Psychoeducational talks for HR, People & Culture, L&D, wellbeing weeks or company events.
  • EAP-style collaboration or referral pathways for employees needing structured psychologically informed support.

Formati izvođenja

  • Online delivery for remote and globally distributed teams.
  • In-person sessions in Barcelona or by arrangement for organizations and events.
  • Hybrid formats combining a live session, reflection exercises and follow-up resources.
  • Content adapted for international and multicultural teams, multicultural audiences and English/Spanish/Serbian-speaking groups.

Primeri tema

  • Burnout prevention before crisis: recognizing patterns, risks and early warning signs.
  • Psychological sustainability at work: boundaries, recovery, availability and performance.
  • Leadership resilience: responsibility, decision pressure and communication under stress.
  • Cross-cultural communication and trust in international and multicultural teams.
  • Remote work, emotional load and connection in distributed teams.
  • Supporting employees through change, uncertainty and adaptation.

Organizacioni ishodi

  • Clearer shared language around stress, burnout risk and sustainable psychological functioning.
  • More practical tools for managers and employees to recognize pressure earlier.
  • Better conversations around boundaries, workload, communication and support.
  • Stronger wellbeing credibility for HR and People teams without generic wellness messaging.
  • A psychologically informed, international perspective on modern radno challenges.

Međunarodna i interkulturalna ekspertiza

  • Experience working across languages, cultures and international professional contexts.
  • Understanding of remote-first work, globally distributed teams and multicultural communication.
  • Support for international professionals navigating relocation, adaptation and identity in demanding roles.
  • A radno mental health approach shaped by psychology, research and real corporate experience.

Invest in radno mental health. Strengthen culture, retention and sustainable performance.

A psychologically credible strategic partner for HR, People & Culture and wellbeing teams.
