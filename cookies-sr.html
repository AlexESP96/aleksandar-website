COOKIE POLICY

How cookies are used.

This page explains what cookies are and how they may be used on this website.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use the site.

2. Types of cookies this website may use

  • Strictly necessary cookies: used for core website functionality and security.
  • Preference cookies: used to remember choices such as display or consent preferences.
  • Analytics cookies: used only if analytics tools are enabled, to understand general website usage.
  • Third-party cookies: may be set by embedded services such as booking, payment, or analytics providers if those tools are added.

3. Consent

Where required by law, non-essential cookies should only be used after you provide consent. You can withdraw or change your consent where cookie controls are available.

4. Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the website function.

5. Updates

This Politika kolačića may be updated if new tools, analytics, booking systems, or payment integrations are added to the website.

6. Kontakt
