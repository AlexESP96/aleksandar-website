FAQ

Common questions about psychological support, workplace wellbeing, referrals, workshops and collaboration.

Do you provide clinical therapy in Spain?

I provide psychological support, counselling-informed support and workplace wellbeing services. I do not currently present myself as a Spanish sanitario psychologist or provider of regulated sanitary psychological treatment in Spain. If someone needs clinical, psychiatric, medical or emergency care, I recommend appropriate specialist support.

Who is individual support suitable for?

Individual support is suitable for adults, professionals and expats navigating work stress, burnout risk, adjustment, relocation, emotional wellbeing, workplace relationships, identity or life transitions, and professional pressure.

When might another referral be more appropriate?

If there is immediate risk, severe mental health crisis, psychiatric need, addiction, eating disorder, complex trauma, or a need for regulated healthcare treatment, I may recommend contacting emergency services, a licensed healthcare provider or a specialized clinician.

How do organizations collaborate with you?

Organizations can start with a workplace wellbeing consultation. From there, collaboration may include workshops, speaking, leadership wellbeing sessions, employee support pathways, referral partnerships or tailored wellbeing initiatives.

Can clinics or psychologists refer clients?

Yes. Clinics, psychologists, counsellors, HR contacts, EAP providers and wellbeing partners may refer adults whose needs relate to workplace stress, burnout risk, adjustment, relocation, professional pressure or emotional wellbeing. Referral boundaries and confidentiality are handled carefully.

What languages are available?

Support is available in English, Spanish and Serbian.

Is support online or in person?

Support is available online. In-person sessions may be available in Barcelona when appropriate.

What is an introductory consultation?

An introductory consultation is a short first conversation to clarify your context, questions, fit and next steps before deciding whether to continue.

Not sure which option fits?

You can start with an introductory consultation or a workplace wellbeing consultation.
