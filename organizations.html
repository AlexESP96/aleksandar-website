For HR decision-makers

What this helps with.

Problems addressed

  • Burnout risk and emotional exhaustion before they become crisis points.
  • Leadership pressure, people responsibility and difficult conversations.
  • Psychological safety, communication strain and workplace conflict.
  • Remote work, cross-cultural collaboration and distributed team pressure.

Available formats

  • 60–90 minute workshops and talks.
  • Multi-session wellbeing or resilience series.
  • Leadership and manager support sessions.
  • EAP-style collaboration and referral pathways.

Why this approach

  • Clinical psychology foundation combined with organizational psychology and counselling practice.
  • PhD research in workplace mental health, wellbeing and organizational psychology.
  • Experience across NGO, public, private and global organizational settings.
  • Warm, practical and research-informed delivery without generic wellbeing messaging.

Expected outcomes

  • Clearer language around stress, burnout and support.
  • Healthier team conversations and stronger manager confidence.
  • More sustainable performance and recovery practices.
  • Better alignment between employee wellbeing and organizational reality.
