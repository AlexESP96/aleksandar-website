Privacy & GDPR

Privacy & GDPR Policy

How personal data is handled when you contact, book, download materials or interact with this website.

Who is responsible for your data

This website and related services are operated by Aleksandar Plavsic, psychologist and workplace wellbeing practitioner based in Barcelona and online. For privacy questions, contact info@aleksandarplavsic.com.

What data may be collected

Depending on how you use the website, I may process your name, email address, phone number, message content, booking details, organization name, referral context, and information you choose to share through contact forms, booking forms, email communication or consultation requests.

Why data is processed

Data is used to respond to enquiries, manage bookings, provide psychological support or workplace wellbeing services, send requested brochures or information, coordinate workshops or organizational collaborations, and maintain appropriate professional records.

Lawful basis

Processing is based on your consent, steps taken before entering a service relationship, performance of a service agreement, legitimate interest in responding to enquiries and managing professional activity, and legal obligations where applicable.

Tools and third parties

The website may use scheduling tools, email services, contact forms, brochure downloads, analytics, hosting providers and embedded third-party services. Only the information necessary for the relevant purpose is processed.

Data minimization and retention

I aim to collect only data that is necessary, relevant and proportionate. Data is retained only for as long as needed for the purpose collected, professional record-keeping, legal obligations or legitimate administrative needs.

Your GDPR rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, or withdrawal of consent where applicable. You may also contact the relevant data protection authority if you believe your rights have not been respected.

Important service boundary

This website describes psychological support, workplace wellbeing and organizational services. It does not present emergency, medical, psychiatric or regulated sanitary psychological treatment in Spain.
