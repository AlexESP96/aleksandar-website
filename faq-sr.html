FAQ

Questions before working together.

Here are the practical details about sessions, confidentiality, booking, payments, and organizaciono support.

How long is an individual session?

Individual sessions are 50 minutes. This is a common format in therapy, coaching, and psychologically informed support because it allows enough time for meaningful work while keeping the session structured.

Are sessions online?

Yes. Sessions are available online by video call. Individual sessions can also be organized in person in Barcelona when suitable, and company sessions can be arranged online or in person by agreement.

What happens in the first session?

The first session is used to understand your situation, clarify what you want support with, identify key patterns, and agree on practical next steps.

Who do you usually work with?

I work with individuals, international clients, LGBTQ+ clients, leaders, clinics, and organizations navigating stress, emotional overwhelm, burnout risk, relationships, communication, identity, pressure, and radno mental health and organizaciono blagostanje.

What experience do you bring?

Moj rad kombinuje obrazovanje iz kliničke psihologije, master iz industrijske i organizacione psihologije, dve godine iskustva u psihološkom savetovanju i individualnoj podršci, četiri godine iskustva u prevenciji samoubistva i emocionalnoj podršci, više od pet godina u međunarodnim korporativnim okruženjima i doktorsko istraživanje mentalnog zdravlja i dobrobiti na radu.

Do you work with companies?

Yes. I support organizations through workshops, organizaciono blagostanje initiatives, liderstvo support, and employee assistance-style services.

Are you LGBTQ+ friendly?

Yes. I aim to provide a respectful, open, and non-judgmental space for LGBTQ+ clients and for people navigating questions around identity, belonging, relationships, and pressure.

Do you collaborate with clinics or wellbeing organizations?

Yes. I am open to collaborations with clinics, EAP providers, coworking communities, startups, universities, and organizations around prevencija burnouta, psychological resilience and wellbeing, workshops, and support for people in demanding environments.

What languages do you work in?

I work in English, Spanish, and Serbian, and I can also assist conversationally in Italian. I try to adapt to clients linguistically whenever possible because expressing yourself in your native or most comfortable language can make support feel clearer, safer, and more personal. The language switcher on the website is available for English, Spanish, and Serbian.

Is everything confidential?

Information shared during sessions is treated confidentially, subject to applicable legal and ethical obligations. Personal data is handled according to the Privacy & GDPR Policy.

How do payments work?

Payment and pricing details are confirmed before booking depending on the type of support, format, and setting. Organizational workshops and collaborations are scoped individually.

Can I reschedule?

Yes. Sessions can be rescheduled with at least 24 hours’ notice. Late cancellations or missed sessions may be charged in full unless exceptional circumstances apply.

Is the 4-session package flexible?

Yes. The package is intended for ongoing support and can be scheduled flexibly, depending on availability.

What if I need urgent help?

If you are going through something urgent, you can reach out to me and I will do my best to respond as soon as possible. However, this service is not an emergency or crisis service. If there is immediate risk or you need urgent clinical support, please contact local emergency services, a crisis hotline, or a licensed clinical provider in your country.
